Geospatial Analysis of Urban Growth

This project involves the analysis of urban expansion over the last two decades using satellite imagery and spatial data. The goal is to assess the impact of urbanization on the environment and identify key areas of development.

Mapping Water Resources

This project focuses on creating interactive maps to visualize water resource availability in various regions. The goal is to provide stakeholders with clear, actionable insights into the distribution of water resources for better planning and management.

Land Use and Zoning Studies

This study analyzes land use patterns in different areas of the city, with a focus on zoning regulations and how land is utilized for residential, commercial, and industrial purposes. The study aims to provide recommendations for urban planning and zoning improvements.
